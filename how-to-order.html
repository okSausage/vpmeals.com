WHY CHOOSE US?

A healthy alternative to a nutritional lifestyle.

Ordering with VP Meal Preps is easy. After viewing our weekly menu selections, choose your meal or meals and give us a call to speak with the chef directly regarding your order. Most order meals for the week, some order daily. If you require a custom meal prep not listed on our menu please let us know and we will do our best to accommodate you. You can either pick-up your order or we will arrange delivery. We are currently accepting four forms of payment options. Cash, Venmo, Cash App and Zelle.
